Stage 1 Breast Cancer: Precautions and Recovery

Precautions for Stage 1 Breast Cancer:

  • Regular follow-ups with your oncologist
  • Adherence to prescribed treatment plans (surgery, radiation, hormone therapy)
  • Healthy lifestyle choices: balanced diet, regular exercise
  • Monitoring for recurrence or new symptoms

Recovery Tips:

  • Emotional support and counseling
  • Physical rehabilitation if needed
  • Support groups and community resources
  • Educational resources on survivorship
